# 327. Dark Theme PDF Zoom Controls

## Summary

PDF previews keep the long-document status and zoom controls visible and
operable in dark theme.

## Behavior

- The page-limit status uses a high-contrast blue pill instead of a near-black
  surface that can blend into dark backgrounds.
- PDF zoom buttons are slightly larger, and the range slider has explicit
  WebKit and Firefox track/thumb styling so it is visibly draggable in the
  desktop WebView.
- Slider changes are accepted on both `input` and `change` events.

## Verification

- Playwright opens the 29-page PDF preview in dark theme, checks status contrast
  against the preview and control backgrounds, verifies the slider is visibly
  sized, and confirms slider, button, and Ctrl-wheel zoom all resize the rendered
  pages without rendering beyond the first 20 pages.
