# 347. Browser Close Response Shrink

Browser tab close responses are authoritative for the tab that initiated the
close request. The local transition guard must continue to reject unrelated
shrinking tab-list events while still accepting the successful close response.

## Contract

- A successful close response may shrink the tab list even while that tab is in
  the local closing state.
- Empty close responses are also accepted when the explicit close operation
  removes the final tab.
- Unsolicited tab-list events that arrive during a local open, close, or
  navigation transition remain guarded against stale shrinkage.

## Coverage

The desktop Browser Playwright coverage closes a named tab and expects the tab
strip to shrink immediately after the successful daemon response.
