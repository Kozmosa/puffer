# 323. Docked PDF Preview Controls

Long and wide PDF previews keep their controls outside the scrollable page
surface. The zoom toolbar and page-limit status remain visible, high-contrast,
and clickable while rendered PDF pages scroll independently in both axes.

Contracts:

- The PDF preview shell owns the visible frame and does not horizontally scroll
  the toolbar.
- Rendered PDF pages scroll inside a dedicated `PDF pages` region.
- Zoom in, zoom out, and reset controls continue to resize already rendered
  canvases without re-rendering the PDF document.
- The page-limit status, such as `Showing first 20 of 29 pages.`, stays in the
  docked control row and must not share the preview background color.

Playwright coverage opens a wide 29-page PDF, scrolls the page region in both
directions, checks that the toolbar does not move, hit-tests the zoom-in button,
and verifies that zoom still changes the rendered page width.
