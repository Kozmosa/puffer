# 359. High-Contrast PDF Page Limit And Zoom Targets

Long PDF previews must keep the page-limit notice visually distinct in the
desktop dark theme and keep zoom controls large enough for reliable pointer
use.

## Contract

- `Showing first N of M pages.` uses a bright status badge in dark mode instead
  of a dark badge that can blend into the preview shell.
- PDF zoom buttons expose at least 44 by 40 px hit targets.
- The reset zoom button remains wide enough to show the icon and percentage
  without compressing adjacent controls.
- The range slider keeps a 40 px tall interactive area.
- Existing 50% to 200% zoom limits, rendered-page cap, keyboard zoom, and
  Ctrl/Command-wheel zoom behavior are unchanged.

## Verification

`tests/files-workspace-ui.spec.ts` opens a 29-page PDF in dark mode, checks the
page-limit badge luminance and contrast, verifies that the badge background is
separate from the shell and control row, and asserts that the zoom-in button has
a desktop-sized hit target before exercising zoom.
