# 335. PDF Preview Zoom Affordance

PDF previews must keep long-document status text visually separate from the
preview surface and make zoom usable from pointer and keyboard input.

Contracts:

- The long-document status is rendered as a high-contrast badge that differs
  from both the PDF canvas background and the controls row in light and dark
  themes.
- The PDF controls row stays pinned above the page scroll region while users
  pan through large or long documents.
- Zoom buttons and the range slider use larger hit targets, and the PDF preview
  also accepts Ctrl/Command plus, minus, and zero for zoom in, zoom out, and
  reset.
- Zoom remains CSS-immediate on already-rendered canvases and preserves the
  20-page render cap for long PDFs.

Playwright coverage opens a 29-page wide PDF, verifies status contrast, uses
the slider, buttons, keyboard shortcuts, scroll pinning, and Ctrl-wheel zoom,
and checks that the rendered page count remains capped.
