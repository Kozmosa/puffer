# 332. Deployment Provider Sync Feedback

The Deployments page must not expose inert top-bar controls. `Sync providers`
is a real interaction with visible progress and completion feedback.

## Contract

- Clicking `Sync providers` immediately disables the button, sets
  `aria-busy=true`, and announces `Syncing providers...` in a live status.
- Completion re-enables the button, clears busy state, and reports how many
  environments and providers were refreshed.
- The status pill stays inside the top bar without increasing its height or
  overlapping the surrounding search and deployment actions.
- The implementation is local to the desktop mock deployment surface until the
  daemon exposes a provider-sync RPC.

## Coverage

Playwright opens Deployments from the sidebar, clicks `Sync providers`, verifies
the busy state and live status, waits for the completion message, and checks the
status pill remains within the top-bar height.
