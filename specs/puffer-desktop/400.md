# 400. PDF Preview Limit Banner And Gesture Zoom

The PDF preview keeps the first-page-limit notice visually separate from the
preview background by rendering it as a warning banner with an explicit page
limit label, strong border, and light warning fill in both themes.

Zoom controls keep larger hit targets and visible plus/minus markers next to the
icons so they remain discoverable in compact file previews.

WebKit/Tauri gesture zoom is handled on the PDF scroll region as well as the
outer renderer. Non-bubbling `gesturestart`/`gesturechange` events on the page
scroller now update the shared zoom state, while bubbling gestures are stopped at
the closest handler to avoid double-applying the scale.
