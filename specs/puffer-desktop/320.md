# PDF Toolbar Hit Target and Status Contrast

The Files PDF preview must keep its controls visually distinct and clickable
even when a large PDF is scrolled both vertically and horizontally.

## Requirements

- The PDF control strip stays above rendered pages with a solid background,
  stronger border, and pointer-enabled hit targets.
- The page-limit status, such as `Showing first 20 of 29 pages.`, uses a
  high-contrast status color that cannot blend into the preview shell
  background.
- Zoom buttons remain clickable after horizontal and vertical scrolling of a
  wide multi-page PDF.

## Verification

Playwright coverage opens a wide 29-page PDF, confirms only the first 20 pages
render, validates the page-limit status contrast against the shell background,
scrolls the PDF preview in both axes, hit-tests the zoom-in button, and verifies
that clicking it immediately increases rendered page width.
