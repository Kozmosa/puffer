# Stripe Webhook Connector Picker

Corbina's pipeline connector catalog includes `stripe-webhook` in the fake
daemon fixture and in Playwright coverage.

## Behavior

- The connector appears in the all-connectors view.
- Searching for `serve webhook`, `setup-only webhook`, or Stripe billing terms
  surfaces the preset.
- The card shows `serve` and `no trigger`, matching setup-only serve-mode
  semantics.
- The details panel shows `skill:stripe-webhook`.
- The staged command is `/connect stripe-webhook stripe-webhook`.

## Compatibility

The fixture count increases to sixteen built-in connectors. Trigger/action
counts are unchanged except for denominators; serve/setup-only webhook counts
include the new Stripe preset.
