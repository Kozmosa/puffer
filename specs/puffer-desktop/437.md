# Preserve completed background turn fallback

## Intent

Avoid losing just-finished background chat output when the daemon announces a
settled turn before the transcript store has caught up.

## Behavior

- `workspace:sessions:changed` with `turn_complete` or `turn_error` still clears
  cached active turn controls such as permission prompts, questions, replay
  deltas, and running/canceling ids.
- Cached assistant, tool, and system timeline items remain available as a
  fallback until the persisted transcript includes them.
- Reopening the background session after a workspace refresh continues to show
  the completed assistant reply and tool activity instead of flashing an empty
  transcript.

## Compatibility

The selected-session settled path still reloads from the daemon; this change only
prevents the background-session transient cache from erasing visible transcript
fallback items too early.
