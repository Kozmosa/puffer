# Contact Candidate Bot Filter

## Design

Contact candidate aggregation now has a final bot-like filter. Candidates whose
ids, names, or retained payload metadata identify them as bots are removed
before list, search, context, or inference sampling can expose them.

## Architecture

`ranked_candidates`, `searched_candidates`, and `sample_inference_candidates`
all reject bot-like candidates. Telegram usernames ending in `bot` are treated
as bots, while non-Telegram candidates are filtered when their local identifier
uses an explicit `bot`, `-bot`, `_bot`, or `.bot` suffix.

## Compatibility

Saved contacts are unchanged. The filter applies only to generated connector
candidates and avoids requiring live connector calls during inference.
