# Contact History Email Display Names

## Design

History-derived contact candidates now avoid using raw email address headers as
display names.

## Architecture

The daemon contact candidate path keeps its Telegram direct-chat title
precedence, then delegates general payload display-name extraction to the
shared subscription contact helper. Address headers such as
`Alice <alice@example.com>` contribute `Alice`, while bare email addresses do
not become contact names.

## Compatibility

The contacts RPC shapes and candidate scoring are unchanged. This only improves
candidate display names before listing, search, context, or inference surfaces
history-derived contacts.
