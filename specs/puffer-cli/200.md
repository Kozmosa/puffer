# Saved Contact Proposal Pruning

## Summary

Saving a contact now removes inferred proposals that contain any of the saved
contact ids before the refreshed contacts snapshot is persisted and returned.

## Design

- The contacts store exposes a proposal pruning helper that normalizes both the
  saved contact ids and proposal ids before comparing them.
- `contacts_save` prunes overlapping proposals after inserting the saved
  contact and before writing `contacts.json`.
- `contacts_list`, `contacts_search`, and save refresh responses therefore stop
  returning suggestions that the user has already promoted to a saved contact.

## Compatibility

Existing stores continue to load unchanged. Deleting a saved contact does not
restore older proposals; users can rerun inference when they want fresh
suggestions.
