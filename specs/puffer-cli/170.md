# Contact Inference and Telegram Ranking Review Fixes

- Contact inference samples the top 30 candidates per contact id prefix before
  invoking the LLM. A prolific connector family can no longer crowd out contacts
  from other enabled connector families.
- Telegram group-chat reply scoring only counts replies and mentions that occur
  in the same chat as the original incoming message. Cross-chat replies cannot
  inflate a group sender's importance.
- The inference prompt may still merge multiple connector ids into one proposed
  contact, but the candidate pool now preserves balanced per-family coverage for
  Telegram, Google, and future prefixed connector ids.
