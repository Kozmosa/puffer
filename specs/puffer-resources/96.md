# Static Seedance Video Catalog Expansion

Date: 2026-06-09

## Scope

Bundled provider resources now enumerate the exact Seedance 2.0 video models
that the existing video adapters can execute.

## Behavior

- BytePlus declares standard `dreamina-seedance-2-0-260128` and fast
  `dreamina-seedance-2-0-fast-260128` video descriptors.
- BytePlus fast limits resolution to `480p` and `720p`; standard keeps
  `480p`, `720p`, and `1080p`.
- Relaydance declares `doubao-seedance-2-0-720p`,
  `doubao-seedance-2-0-1080p`, and `doubao-seedance-2-0-fast-260128`.
- Relaydance resolution-specific model ids expose resolution as a single-value
  parameter, while fast exposes `480p` and `720p`.
- Video discovery remains `static`; no runtime video model discovery is added.

## Governance

Catalog tests assert exact model ids, static discovery, executable adapters,
valid defaults, and model-specific resolution boundaries for BytePlus and
Relaydance.
