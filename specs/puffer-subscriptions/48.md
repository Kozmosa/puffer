# puffer-subscriptions update 48

Monitor bindings suppress muted Telegram notification events.

- The router treats bindings whose slug starts with `monitor-`, or triage-agent
  bindings described as monitors, as monitor bindings.
- Monitor bindings do not match events whose payload has
  `notification_muted=true` or `notification_silent=true`.
- Other workflow bindings continue to receive those events, so explicit
  append, SQLite, connector action, or custom triage workflows keep their
  configured behavior.

This keeps the mute policy scoped to `/monitor` without dropping connector
events globally.
