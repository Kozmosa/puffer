# Email Connector Action Surface

## Summary

The built-in `email` and `gmail-browser` connector templates now advertise a
shared email action surface for `ConnectorAct`.

## Actions

- `list_emails`, `list_inbox`, `list_category`, and `search_emails` read email
  rows with optional mailbox/category/label, keyword, sender, subject, unread,
  limit, and scan-limit filters.
- `mark_read` marks selected messages as read.
- `draft_reply` and `draft_forward` create draft messages.
- `send_email` sends addressed email with optional `cc`, `bcc`,
  `in_reply_to`, and `references`.
- `delete` deletes selected messages.
- The legacy `send_message` action remains present on `email` for compatibility.

## Permissions

Read-only list/search actions use `external_message_read` with
`external_side_effect = false`. Mutating actions use side-effecting categories:
`external_message_interaction`, `external_message_draft`,
`external_message_send`, and `external_message_delete`.

## Runtime Contract

Subscriber-backed actions are correlated by the requested `action` field for
`email_act` and `gmail_browser_act` custom commands, so concurrent terminal
events cannot satisfy the wrong command wait.
