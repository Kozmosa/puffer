# Telegram Bot Contact Extraction

## Design

Shared Telegram contact-id extraction now ignores bot-looking usernames even
when older payloads lack `chat_is_bot` or `sender_is_bot` flags.

## Logic

Telegram `chat_username` and `sender_username` values ending in `bot` are not
converted into `telegram@...` contact ids. Explicit Telegram bot flags still
take precedence and short-circuit extraction.

## Compatibility

The normalized contact-id format is unchanged. This only removes bot accounts
from generated contact ids derived from Telegram payloads.
