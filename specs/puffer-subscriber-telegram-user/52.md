# Live Update Stream Recovery

- The Telegram personal-account subscriber treats transient live update stream
  failures as recoverable when Telegram or Grammers reports unknown update
  constructors, bad responses, MSGID decrease retries, or transport failures.
- Recoverable stream failures are still recorded in connection diagnostics, but
  with `fatal=false`; auth invalidation remains fatal and still requires
  `/connect telegram-login telegram-user`.
- On recovery, the subscriber reconnects from the persisted session, refreshes
  the delivery cursor for the current account, rehydrates startup state, and
  resumes live update delivery without forcing the supervisor to mark the
  connection degraded.
