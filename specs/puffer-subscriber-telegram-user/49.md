# Saved Contact Username Resolution

## Summary

Startup hydration now resolves saved `telegram@username` contact IDs through
Telegram itself and writes the returned profile metadata into `peer-cache.json`.

## Design

- `SkillEnv` accepts an optional `workspace_config_dir` from
  `PUFFER_WORKSPACE_CONFIG_DIR`.
- Hydration reads `.puffer/runtime/contacts.json` when the workspace config dir
  is available.
- Public Telegram handles from saved contact IDs are resolved with
  `Client::resolve_username`.
- Resolved chats are observed into the peer cache, and their profile photo is
  fetched through the existing avatar downloader when Telegram exposes one.

## Compatibility

Conversation history remains useful for contact IDs and relationship context,
but Telegram profile names and avatars come from Telegram resolution instead of
conversation text. Missing, private, invalid, or bot-like usernames are skipped
without failing startup hydration.
