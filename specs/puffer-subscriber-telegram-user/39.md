# Fast Telegram difference deadline

## Summary

Puffer vendors `grammers-session` 0.7 and lowers its no-update difference
deadline from 15 minutes to 2 seconds for the Telegram personal-account
subscriber.

## Design

- The patch keeps Grammers' normal update-state machine and uses Telegram's
  official `updates.getDifference` / `updates.getChannelDifference` paths.
- The shorter deadline covers dialogs that do not reliably arrive as pushed
  socket updates, including muted or low-priority groups.
- The subscriber still suppresses muted/silent messages at delivery time, but
  the cursor now observes them promptly instead of discovering them minutes
  later.

## Compatibility

This is a crate patch for Puffer's build only. It does not alter subscriber
event schemas or login/session file formats, but it increases Telegram update
difference RPC frequency while the subscriber is idle.
