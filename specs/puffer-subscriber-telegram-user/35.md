# puffer-subscriber-telegram-user update 35

Telegram notification suppression now includes global notification scopes in
addition to per-dialog and per-peer settings. The subscriber refreshes
`NotifyUsers`, `NotifyChats`, and `NotifyBroadcasts` during startup hydration
and peer-hash priming, applies later global `UpdateNotifySettings` socket
updates, and treats messages as suppressed when either the chat itself or its
global scope is muted.

This fixes inherited mute cases where a dialog's own `notify_settings` reports
unmuted while Telegram Desktop still presents the group/channel as muted due to
the account-wide notification category.
